About me

I take pleasure in tackling challenges in medical research and learning about new diseases or treatments, and I have particularly enjoyed the variety of writing and communication projects I have done in the past. I am now keen on exploring opportunities where I can focus on these aspects.

I have a PhD in Medicine and a MSc/Graduate Engineer Diploma in Chemistry. My background is in vascular health, diabetes, neuroscience, and Covid-19, and I have professional experience in academia and industry (pharmaceutic, cosmetic, and chemistry).

I have a strong experience in technical and creative writing, both in English and in French, and in oral communication targetted at a wide range of audiences. I have also done a lot of teamwork and managed projects with multiple collaborators in international and interdisciplinary environments.
